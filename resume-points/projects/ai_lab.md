---
project_name: "AI-Powered Research Lab Workflow Automation API"
---

- Developed a multi-tenant Rails API to manage researchers, experiments, and compliance workflows, implementing rolebased access control and audit logging to support regulated research environments. [Ruby on Rails, API]
- Developed a lifecycle and compliance system with protocol versioning, document management in ActiveStorage and S3, and Sidekiq background job processing for deadline reminders and real-time notifications. [ActiveStorage, S3, Sidekiq]
- Integrated OpenAI APIs with RAG architecture and vector embeddings to enable semantic search across experiment logs and compliance documents to enhance compliance risk detection. [OpenAI API, RAG, pgvector]
